让你记住window.location的那些属性
下面以输入http://localhost:63342/dev/tools/useful-JavaScript-Fragment/window.location.html/?id=aaa/#bbb为例

输出结果如下:
window.location.href: http://localhost:63342/dev/tools/useful-JavaScript-Fragment/window.location.html/?id=aaa/#bbb
window.location.protocol: http:
window.location.host: localhost:63342
window.location.hostname: localhost
window.location.port: 63342
window.location.pathname: /dev/tools/useful-JavaScript-Fragment/window.location.html/
window.location.hash: #bbb
window.location.search: ?id=aaa/
